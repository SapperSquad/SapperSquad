### 🛠️ What I make

Small, focused mods and data packs for **Minecraft 1.21.1** — all sharing one idea: take a vanilla loop you already know (farming, hauling, fighting, fishing) and give it depth worth automating.

| Project | What it does |
|---|---|
| **⛏️ PhytoForge** | Grow your ores. Crop-based multiplication that turns farming into your smelter's best friend — with strains reaching all the way to platinum and chromium. |
| **🔧 Forgework** | Pipes, power, and a full material tier climb (Bronze → Steel → Invar → Tungsten → Titanium). Move resources and generate energy — and it plays nice with your existing FE network. |
| **🔫 Gunsmith** | Firearms with weight-class handling and tungsten armor-piercing rounds. No jamming, no durability grind — just shooting. |
| **🏛️ Forged** *(data pack)* | New worldgen structures to discover across your world. Pure vanilla — no mods required. |
| **🎣 Reel Rivals** *(data pack)* | Competitive fishing for multiplayer servers. Turn the dock into a leaderboard. |
